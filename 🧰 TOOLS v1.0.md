**Look for principles, not prose.**

**Does it vibrate with Compact truth?**

- Feels like "oh, obviously"
- Connects to multiple Compact articles
- Survives inversion questioning
- Works across beings

#### STEP 3: DISTILLATION
**Compress to essence without losing signal.**

**Rules:**
- One clear sentence per principle
- Remove qualifiers unless they're the point
- Keep living metaphors that teach
- Make actionable when possible

**Example:**
- Raw: "You know what I think is that basically like clarification is probably the main thing that helps you get out of traps and stuff"
- Distilled: "Clarification dissolves traps."

#### STEP 4: INTEGRATION MAP
**Note where it could live in the architecture.**

**Ask:**
- Which Compact article does this echo?

- New principle or demonstration of existing?
